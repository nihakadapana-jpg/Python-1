How to Use

Step 1: Encryption

  • Go to the Encryption page.
  • Select one of our suggested images or upload your own.
  • Enter the message you want to hide in the text area.
  • Enter a secret key. Remember this key! You will need it to decrypt the message later.
  • Click "Encrypt & Hide Data".
  • Download the resulting image. It looks like a normal image but contains your secret!

Step 2: Decryption

  • Go to the Decryption page.
  • Upload the image that you previously encrypted.
  • Enter the same secret key you used during encryption.
  • Click "Decrypt & Extract Data" to see your hidden message.
